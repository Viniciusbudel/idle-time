# Atomic Age Worker Prompts (Pixel Art)

Here are the prompts for generating **Atomic Age** workers, matching the specific names defined in the game code (`worker_name_registry.dart`).

**Style:** Pixel art icon, 64x64, clean cute style (matching Roaring 20s assets).
**References:** Retrofuturism, Fallout, Bioshock, 1950s Sci-Fi.

## Common: Lab Assistant
> Lab Assistant, Atomic Age era junior researcher, pixel art icon, 64x64, clean cute style. Character wearing a pristine white lab coat over a blue jumpsuit (Vault-Tec style) and large round black-rimmed glasses, holding a clipboard and pen. Nervous but eager expression, messy hair. Retrofuturism aesthetic, Fallout 4 vibe. White, soft blue, and sterile grey tones.

## Rare: Reactor Tech
> Reactor Tech, Atomic Age era industrial technician, pixel art icon, 64x64, clean cute style. Character wearing heavy rubber gloves and a lead apron over a jumpsuit, holding a clicking geiger counter. Focused expression, bulky safety goggles pulled down over eyes (Bioshock Big Daddy vibe). Industrial yellow, hazard black, and steel grey tones. Heavy retro-industrial look.

## Epic: Isotope Handler
> Isotope Handler, Atomic Age era hazardous material specialist, pixel art icon, 64x64, clean cute style. Character wearing a bulky retro sci-fi hazmat suit with the visor flipped up, holding a glowing green fuel rod with tongs. Confident smirk, radioactive green glow reflecting on face (Fallout Glowing One aesthetic). Neon green and metallic silver tones.

## Legendary: Nuclear Physicist
> Nuclear Physicist, Atomic Age era brilliant scientist, pixel art icon, 64x64, clean cute style. Character wearing a brown tweed suit under an open lab coat, wild white hair (Einstein style), holding an atom model and a piece of chalk. Manic grin, eyes wide with discovery. Bioshock scientist aesthetic, Art Deco glasses. Chalkboard green and tweed brown tones.

## Paradox: Quantum Splitter
> Quantum Splitter, Atomic Age era reality-bending entity, pixel art icon, 64x64, clean cute style. Character wearing a cutting-edge containment suit that is "glitching" or phasing into pixels at the edges (Bioshock Infinite Tear effect). Holding a miniature unstable black hole in hand. Eyes are swirling purple vortexes. Deep Paradox Purple, Void Black, and electric blue tones.
